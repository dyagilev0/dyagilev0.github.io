Naming is a baby-name reference app that helps you explore names, meanings, origins, and popularity. For any privacy questions, contact us at ava.app.names@gmail.com.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or postal address to use the App. We do not collect your contacts, photos, messages, precise location, or health data.

4. Advertising & tracking (App Tracking Transparency)

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. With your permission, AdMob may use your device’s advertising identifier to show more relevant (personalized) ads. On first use, iOS will ask whether you allow the App to track you across apps and websites (App Tracking Transparency). If you decline, ads are still shown but are non-personalized. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. In-app purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive or store your payment card details. We may receive confirmation that a purchase occurred to enable the corresponding features.

6. How information is used

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • To measure usage and understand how features are used (analytics).
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability and performance.
  • To display advertising, including, with consent, personalized ads.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the third-party service providers listed above, solely for the purposes described, and subject to their privacy policies.

8. Data retention

Data collected by our third-party partners is retained according to their respective retention policies. You may request deletion of data associated with your device by contacting us.

9. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for adults (for example, expectant parents) and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at ava.app.names@gmail.com. You can also limit ad tracking through your device settings as described above.
